    <p><strong>Khanna</strong> is a family-owned restaurant dedicated to bringing authentic and flavorful cuisine to our community. 
    Our goal is to provide delicious meals, a welcoming atmosphere, and an experience that combines culture, tradition, and quality.</p>
    <p>Founded in <span class="highlight">2001</span>, Khanna began as a small local eatery with a focus on fresh ingredients and 
    traditional recipes passed down through generations. Over the years, our restaurant has become a beloved destination 
    for food lovers, celebrated for our <u>homemade dishes</u> and exceptional customer service.</p>
    <p>In <span class="special">2022</span>, Khanna expanded under new ownership and management. While we grew and modernized, 
    we remained committed to our roots, keeping our signature menu items and maintaining a warm, family-friendly environment.</p>
    <p>Our philosophy is simple: <em>serve fresh, authentic food and make every guest feel at home</em>. Every dish is prepared 
    with care, from scratch-made sauces to carefully selected ingredients, ensuring a high-quality dining experience.</p>
